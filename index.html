FOUNDER & CEO

Building A Vision From Lesotho To The World.

Nthatuoa Bernica Leaba is the Founder and CEO of KAYRO, a technology and innovation company founded with a vision to create meaningful digital solutions and opportunities.

Her entrepreneurial journey began with limited financial resources, but with a strong willingness to learn, build, and create. Using a phone, data, self-directed learning, and determination, she began developing the foundation of KAYRO step by step.

The journey represents a commitment to building with what is available while continuously working toward greater possibilities.

Through KAYRO, the vision is to build a globally recognized company from Africa that uses technology, education, innovation, and entrepreneurship to empower people and businesses.
